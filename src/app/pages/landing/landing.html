Who We Are

About GoldenRoyal Legal Practitioners

We are a firm of barristers and solicitors that are passionately devoted to meeting your personal and business legal needs. We are particularly fervid about applying international best practices in solving your legal problems and challenges. Our Lawyers are not just committed to proffering tested and trusted solutions to legal problems, we are focused on providing proven solutions that suit individual legal entities without attracting additional liabilities.

Our litigation attorneys understand the Nigerian judicial system and have devised strategies that are not only aimed at navigating the system successfully, but that also help you get to the end of your matters without much delay. While our knowledge of strategies that produce the best results at both trial and appellate courts is top-notch, we understand that our clients' time is precious, necessitating the adoption of an approach that sets the court as a last resort.

Our corporate law attorneys are versed in the laws that regulate business activities in Nigeria. They have wealth of experience in diverse business areas such as taxation, company secretarial, contracts, real estate law, intellectual property law, entertainment law, sports law and immigration. We believe that our depth of knowledge will help your business successfully navigate the Nigerian business environment without stress.
